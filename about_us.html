Mission:

It is been said that one lives numerous lives, when one reads books.

In today's world of speed, internet, television and malls, the habit of reading books has taken a hit. This can be attributed to the following reasons:

  1. No time to go browse through Book shops or Libraries.
  2. Lack of libraries in the vicinity
  3. Old library system with its difficulty to navigate and browse
  4. Books have become very expensive to purchase
  5. Advent of the Internet / TV as alternate sources of entertainment

Through this venture we intend to make reading books a hassle free, cost effective and pleasurable experience. All you have to do is just sign up, choose a membership plan and order the books that you want to read. We deliver the books to your doorstep, free of cost. After you finish reading, just inform us and we will deliver the next books and do a free pick of the previous set of books.

Who Are We:

Omkar Bhosale and Onkar Chakrawar are the founders of Books2Door.com. It was our love and passion about books which made us to start this venture. We will be soon launching our venture in more than 150 cities which also include Mumbai, Chennai, Delhi and Kolkata.
